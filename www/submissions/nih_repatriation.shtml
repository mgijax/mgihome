Submit Data on Knockouts for Possible Repatriation

To make existing mouse strains carrying targeted knockouts (nulls and conditionals) more accessible to the scientific community, NIH is facilitating the placement of these stocks into public repositories. The purpose of this form is to gather information from investigators who have developed and/or maintain knockout mice that can be transferred to public repositories for wider distribution to the scientific community.

Helpful resources for using this form:

  • Genes for which one or more knockout allele(s) have been reported
    (These lists are limited to gene knockouts and conditional knockouts in the public domain. They do not include genes that have other types of genetically engineered alleles or mutations)
