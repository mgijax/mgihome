The purpose of this inventory is to determine how many (and which) genes in the mouse have been knocked out to make null alleles and in what repositories or laboratories the resulting mice or cell lines reside. The information you provide will be used in conjunction with data from MGI and mouse repository resources for developing potential plans to produce a knockout of every gene.

  • Information you provide will be held privately unless you indicate on this form that it can be publicly released.
  • If you have many knockout alleles to describe, please e-mail mgi-help@informatics.jax.org for instructions on bulk data transfer.
  • If you have any questions for NIH staff working on this project, please send e-mail to komp@mail.nih.gov.
  • More information is available on the the Frequently Asked Questions page.
  • Thank you for helping with this survey!
