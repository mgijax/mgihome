Nominate Knockouts for Placement in Public Repositories

To make existing mouse strains carrying targeted knockouts (nulls and conditionals) more accessible to the scientific community, NIH is facilitating the placement of these stocks into public repositories. The purpose of this form is to gather information from the scientific community to determine which of these knockout stocks are of highest interest, and therefore of highest priority, for placing in repositories.

Helpful resources for using this form:

  • Genes for which one or more knockout allele(s) have been reported
    (These lists are limited to gene knockouts and conditional knockouts in the public domain. They do not include genes that have other types of genetically engineered alleles or mutations)
    • candidate genes to nominate for repatriating mice to public repositories.
      Mice carrying knockouts of these genes have been created, but are not in a public repository.
    • available knockouts.
      Mice carrying knockouts of these genes have been created, and are available through public repositories.
    • full list of knockouts.
      Combination of the above two lists, showing genes that have been knocked-out or conditionally knocked-out.
