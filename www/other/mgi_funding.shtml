Mouse Genome Database (MGD) Project

MGD is supported by program project grant HG00330 entitled "Mouse Genome Informatics", awarded to Janan T. Eppig from the National Human Genome Research Institute (NHGRI) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH).

Gene Expression Database (GXD) Project

GXD is supported by grant HD33745 entitled "Gene Expression Database for Mouse Development", awarded to Martin Ringwald from the National Institute of Child Health and Human Development (NICHD) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH).

Mouse Genome Sequence (MGS) Project (MGS)

MGS is supported by contract FG02-99ER62850 entitled "Informatics for Mouse-Human Comparative Genomics", awarded to Carol J. Bult from the Office of Science of the Department of Energy (DOE).

Mouse Tumor Biology Database (MTB) Project

MTB is supported by a contract 97CXS0228A entitled "Electronic Access to Mouse Tumor Data", awarded to Janan T. Eppig from the National Cancer Institute (NCI) of the National Insitutes of Health (NIH).

Rat Genome Data (RGD) Project

RGD is supported through a subcontract to Janan T. Eppig. It is funded by grant HL64541 entitled "Rat Genome Database", awarded to Dr. Howard J.Jacob of the Medical College of Wisconsin from the National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute (NHBLI) of the National Insitutes of Health (NIH).
