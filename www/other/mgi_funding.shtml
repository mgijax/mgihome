MGI Current Funding Support

Mouse Genome Database (MGD) Project

MGD is supported by program project grant HG000330, entitled "Mouse Genome Informatics," awarded to Janan T. Eppig from the National Human Genome Research Institute (NHGRI) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH). The MGD co-PIs are Judith A. Blake, Staff Scientist; Carol Bult, Staff Scientist; James A. Kadin, Senior Research Scientist; and Joel E. Richardson, Senior Research Scientist.

Gene Expression Database (GXD) Project

GXD is supported by grant HD033745, entitled "Gene Expression Database for Mouse Development," awarded to Martin Ringwald from the National Institute of Child Health and Human Development (NICHD) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH). The GXD co-PIs are Janan T. Eppig, Senior Staff Scientist; James A. Kadin, Senior Research Scientist; and Joel E. Richardson, Senior Research Scientist.

Mouse Tumor Biology Database (MTB) Project

MTB is supported by grant CA089713, entitled "Electronic Access to Mouse Tumor Data," awarded to Janan T. Eppig from the National Cancer Institute (NCI) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH). The MTB co-PIs are John P. Sundberg, Senior Staff Scientist and Carol Bult, Staff Scientist.

Gene Ontology (GO) Project

GO is supported by grant HG002273, entitled "Gene Ontology Consortium," awarded to Judith A. Blake from the National Human Genome Research Institute (NHGRI) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH). The Gene Ontology (GO) consortium funds GO activities at eight institutions, including The Jackson Laboratory.

MouseCyc Project

MouseCyc is supported by grant HG003622, entitled "MouseCyc: A Biochemical Pathway Database for the Mouse," awarded to Carol J. Bult from the National Human Genome Research Institute (NHGRI) of the National INstitutes of Health (NIH).
