Mouse Genome Database (MGD) Project
 
MGD is supported by program project grant HG000330 from the National Human Genome Research Institute (NHGRI) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH), entitled "Mouse Genome Informatics," awarded to the multi-PI team of Carol J. Bult, Professor and Judith A. Blake, Professor. MGD co-PIs are James A. Kadin, Senior Research Scientist, Joel E. Richardson, Senior Research Scientist, and Cynthia L. Smith, Research Scientist.
Gene Expression Database (GXD) Project
 
GXD is supported by grant HD062499, entitled "Gene Expression Database for Mouse Development," awarded to Associate Professor Martin Ringwald from the Eunice Kennedy Shriver National Institute of Child Health and Human Development (NICHD) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH). The GXD co-PIs are James A. Kadin, Senior Research Scientist and Joel E. Richardson, Senior Research Scientist.
Mouse Tumor Biology Database (MTB) Project
 
MTB is supported by grant CA089713, entitled "Electronic Access to Mouse Tumor Data," awarded to Professor Carol Bult from the National Cancer Institute (NCI) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH). The MTB co-PI is John P. Sundberg, Professor.
Gene Ontology (GO) Project
 
GO is supported by grant HG002273, entitled "Gene Ontology Consortium," awarded to Professor Judith A. Blake from the National Human Genome Research Institute (NHGRI) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH). The Gene Ontology (GO) consortium funds GO activities at eight institutions, including The Jackson Laboratory.
