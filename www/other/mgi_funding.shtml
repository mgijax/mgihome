MGI Funding Support

Mouse Genome Database (MGD) Project

MGD is supported by program project grant HG00330, entitled "Mouse Genome Informatics," awarded to Janan T. Eppig from the National Human Genome Research Institute (NHGRI) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH).

Gene Expression Database (GXD) Project

GXD is supported by grant HD33745, entitled "Gene Expression Database for Mouse Development," awarded to Martin Ringwald from the National Institute of Child Health and Human Development (NICHD) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH).

Mouse Genome Sequence (MGS) Project

The MGS Project is funded by contract DE-FG02-99ER62850, entitled "Informatics for Mouse-Human Comparative Genomics," awarded to Carol J. Bult by the U. S. Department of Energy Office of Science.

Mouse Tumor Biology Database (MTB) Project

MTB is supported by grant CA89713, entitled "Electronic Access to Mouse Tumor Data," awarded to Janan T. Eppig from the National Cancer Institute (NCI) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH).

Rat Genome Data Project

The Rat Genome Data project is supported through a subcontract to Janan T. Eppig. It is funded by grant HL64541, entitled "Rat Genome Database", awarded to Dr. Howard J.Jacob of the Medical College of Wisconsin from the National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute (NHBLI) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH).

Gene Ontology (GO) Project

The Gene Ontology project is supported by grant HG002273, entitled "Gene Ontology Consortium," awarded to Judith A. Blake, from the National Human Genome Research Institute (NHGRI) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH).
