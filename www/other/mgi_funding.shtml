Mouse Genome Database (MGD) Project
 
MGD is supported by program project grant HG000330, entitled "Mouse Genome Informatics," awarded to Professor Janan T. Eppig from the National Human Genome Research Institute (NHGRI) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH). The MGD co-PIs are Judith A. Blake, Associate Professor; Carol Bult, Associate Professor; James A. Kadin, Senior Research Scientist; and Joel E. Richardson, Senior Research Scientist.
Gene Expression Database (GXD) Project
 
GXD is supported by grant HD033745, entitled "Gene Expression Database for Mouse Development," awarded to Associate Professor Martin Ringwald from the National Institute of Child Health and Human Development (NICHD) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH). The GXD co-PIs are Janan T. Eppig, Professor; James A. Kadin, Senior Research Scientist; and Joel E. Richardson, Senior Research Scientist.
Mouse Tumor Biology Database (MTB) Project
 
MTB is supported by grant CA089713, entitled "Electronic Access to Mouse Tumor Data," awarded to Professor Janan T. Eppig from the National Cancer Institute (NCI) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH). The MTB co-PIs are John P. Sundberg, Professor and Carol Bult, Associate Professor.
Gene Ontology (GO) Project
 
GO is supported by grant HG002273, entitled "Gene Ontology Consortium," awarded to Associate Professor Judith A. Blake from the National Human Genome Research Institute (NHGRI) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH). The Gene Ontology (GO) consortium funds GO activities at eight institutions, including The Jackson Laboratory.
