Mouse Facts  

The "laboratory mouse" originated from a mixture of M. m. domesticus and M. m. musculus subspecies. An inbred strain of the laboratory mouse is defined as a strain that has been brother/sister mated for 20 or more consecutive generations and can be traced to a single ancestral pair. For further information about inbred strains of mice see the Strains and Polymorphisms page.
