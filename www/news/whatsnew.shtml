May, 2003

MGI 2.96 Release: May 22, 2003
We've added an Adult Mouse Anatomy Browser to our vocabulary browsers, the ability to query by Interpro terms or by RIKEN and IMAGE clone collections to our query forms, and links to PDB from our gene detail pages.

April, 2003

MGI 2.95 Release: April 1, 2003
Explore the new Mammalian Phenotype Browser, try out the new nomenclature search from our Genes and Markers Query Form, and respond to the MGI Survey.

March, 2003

Revised Mutation Submission form
We've revised the MGI Mutation Submission form to make it more comprehensive and to include examples of additional strain background information and phenotype descriptions.
