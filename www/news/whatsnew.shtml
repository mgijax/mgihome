Past Release Announcements

December, 2003
MGI 2.98 Release: December 11, 2003  
MGI is pleased to announce its 2.98 release. This release consists of performance enhancements and bug fixes.


November, 2003
Update: November 17, 2003
We now send e-mail acknowledgements summarizing all new or revised data from our Mouse Strain Registry and Mutation Submission forms. Users will have an exact record of any submissions and a contact address for any questions.

MGI 2.97 Release: November 5, 2003
This release features several enhancements to our Gene Expression Database(GXD) including two new query forms.

May, 2003
MGI 2.96 Release: May 22, 2003
We've added an Adult Mouse Anatomy Browser to our vocabulary browsers, the ability to query by Interpro terms or by RIKEN and IMAGE clone collections to our query forms, and links to PDB from our gene detail pages.

April, 2003
MGI 2.95 Release: April 1, 2003
Explore the new Mammalian Phenotype Browser, try out the new nomenclature search from our Genes and Markers Query Form, and respond to the MGI Survey.

March, 2003
Revised Mutation Submission form
We've revised the MGI Mutation Submission form to make it more comprehensive and to include examples of additional strain background information and phenotype descriptions.

January, 2003
MGI and RIKEN collaborated in the Phase I release of FANTOM2 data.

An article on the Mouse Genome Database appeared in NAR's 2003 database issue by Blake JA, Richardson JE, Bult CJ, Kadin JA, Eppig JT, and the members of the Mouse Genome Database Group. See PubMed.
