April 1, 2010: MGI announces the following enhancements for its 4.33 release:
 
The MGI Batch Query can now handle very large files.
Gene detail pages now include notes for any MGI marker associations to VEGA, Ensembl or NCBI gene models with gene/pseudogene discrepancies.
Gene detail pages may also include strain specificity information (e.g., when a gene is not present in a given strain), as well as links to protein ontology and gene model evidence for a given marker.
A new MGI Data and Statistical Report provides details about the gene vs. pseudogene discrepancies exists.
