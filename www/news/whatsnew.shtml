Past Release Announcements
April 8, 2005
Version 3.12 of MGI adds a paragraph of summary information to the Gene Ontology (GO) Annotation Detail page, as well as performance enhancements and bug fixes.
February, 2005
Version 3.11 of MGI adds the ability to query by rat and human synonyms, a redesign of the MGI Glossary, and enhancements to performance.
January, 2005
Latest versions of
Rules for Nomenclature of Genes, Genetic Markers, Alleles, and Mutations in Mouse and Rat
Rules for Nomenclature of Mouse and Rat Strains
Rules for Nomenclature of Chromosome Aberrations

December, 2004
Version 3.1 of MGI adds sequence assembly coordinates from the NCBI and the Ensembl Gene Model. The coordinates are now integrated with mouse sequences from GenBank, RefSeq, SWISS-PROT, TrEMBL, the public mouse genome assembly, and the TIGR, DoTS, and NIA mouse gene indices.
November, 2004
Version 3.02 of MGI adds mouse cDNA clones from the I.M.A.G.E. Consortium and the National Institute on Aging (NIA, NIA 15K and NIA 7.4K) sets.
October, 2004
