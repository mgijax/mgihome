  • Mouse Genome Monthly Newsletter - January 21, 2002
    Issue #3 of the newsletter is now available online.

  • UCSC Human Genome Browser, January 21, 2002
    There is now a link from human genes represented in the Browser to gene records of mouse orthologs in MGI. Thanks to David Haussler and Jim Kent for adding this link the UCSC resource.

  • Glycine Receptor, Alpha Family joins the Gene Family Reports, January 21, 2002.

  • Articles describing resources available through the Mouse Genome Informatics web site appear in recent issues of Nucleic Acids Research.
    Blake JA, Richardson JE, Bult CJ, Kadin JA, Eppig JT and the Mouse Genome Database Group. 2002. The Mouse Genome Database (MGD): The Model Organism Database for the Laboratory Mouse. Nucl Acids Res 30: 113-115.

    Bult C, Krupke D, N, Näf D, Sundberg J, and Eppig, JT. Web-based access to mouse models of human cancers: the Mouse Tumor Biology (MTB) Database. 2001. Nucl Acids Res 29: 95-97.
