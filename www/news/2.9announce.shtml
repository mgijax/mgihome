MGI Release 2.9 Announcement - November 11, 2002

The MGI 2.9 release features:

  • Look and Feel
    • New look for MGI Home: Help page, Resources, About MGI, What's New
    • Toolbar
    • Marker detail page - tase of what's to come ...
    • New summary reports: sequence summary, gene ontology,m



  • Links to Ensembl Database

    MGI now provides links to Ensembl mouse gene models from MGI markers that share a common external sequence accession ID with those models. We also obtain chromosome map locations for MGI genes associated with Ensemb gene models. Ensembl is a joint project between the European Bioinformatics Institute/European Molecular Biology Laboratory (EMBL-EBI) and the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute.
