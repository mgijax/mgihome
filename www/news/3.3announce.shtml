MGI 3.3 Release Announcement

Version 3.3 of MGI provides the scientific community with significant new information about potential mouse models of human diseases. The database contains Online Mendelian Inheritance in Man (OMIM) descriptions of human diseases, syndromes, and conditions, and information about genes thought to be associated with the diseases. Based on orthologies between mouse and human genes and data from researchers about mouse phenotypes resembling human diseases and the genotypes of those mice, MGI can establishes relationship between mouse and human disease phenotypes. The new data are integrated with the rich biological knowledge represented for mouse genes and strains in MGI, allowing exploration of sequence, expression, orthology, and other related data.

GXD adds data from two large-scale in situ hybridization screens

MGI's Gene Expression Database (GXD) is pleased to announce the inclusion of data from two major in situ hybridization studies.

Gray PA; Fu H; Luo P; Zhao Q; Yu J; Ferrari A; Tenzen T; Yuk DI; Tsung EF; Cai Z; Alberta JA; Cheng LP; Liu Y; Stenman JM; Valerious MT; Billings N; Kim HA; Greenberg ME; McMahon AP; Rowitch DH; Stiles CD; Ma Q. Mouse Brain Organization Revealed Through Direct Genome Scale TF Expression Analysis. 2004. Science 306:2255-2257.

Dr. Paul Gray and colleagues examined the expression of putative transcription factors in the developing mouse nervous system.

You can view the data set at accession number J:91257.

It includes:

  • 1332 genes
  • 1505 probes
  • 2766 assays (in situ hybridizations at E10.5, E13.5 and P0 stages)
Blackshaw S; Harpavat S; Trimarchi J; Cai L; Huang H; Kuo WP; Weber G; Lee K; Fraioli RE; Cho SH; Yung R; Asch E; Ohno-Machado L; Wong WH; Cepko CL. Genomic Analysis of Mouse Retinal Development. 2004. PloS Biol 2:E247.

Dr. Seth Blackshaw and colleagues examined the expression of genes in the developing retina.

You can view the data set at accession number J:93300.

It includes:

  • 1013 genes
  • 1025 probes
  • 1028 assays (in situ hybridizations at E12.5, E14.5, E16.5, E18.5, P0-1, P2-3, P4-5, P6-7, P8-10 and Adult stages)
