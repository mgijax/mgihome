  • Phosphatidylinositol-4-phosphate 5-kinase family joins the Gene Family Reports
    The phosphatidylinositol-4-phosphate 5-kinase family of enzymes catalyze the phosphorylation of phosphatidylinositol-4-phosphate to form phosphatidylinositol-4,5-bisphosphate.

    June, 2002

  • Synaptotagmin family joins the Gene Family Reports
    The synaptotagmins are a large family of genes thought to mediate synaptic function.

    May, 2002

  • Annexin family Joins Gene Family Reports
    The annexins are calcium-dependent membrane-binding proteins involved in the regulation of membrane and cytoskeletal events and in the inhibition of inflammation and coagulation.

    April, 2002

  • Revised Rules for Nomenclature of Mouse and Rat Strains
    These new and revised rules for strain nomenclature reflect a collaboration between the International Committee on Standardized Nomenclature for Mice and the Rat Genome and Nomenclature Committee and are applicable to strains of both species.

  • Issue #4 April 2002 of the Mouse Genome Monthly Newsletter
    The new public mouse assembly is released!

  • Calcium Channel, Voltage-dependent, Gamma Subunit joins the Gene Family Reports
    Ca(2+) channels play a central role in a wide range of secretory processes. Ca(2+) channels consist of a complex of protein subunits including gamma subunits which modulate channel properties such as inactivation and channel targeting.

  • MGI Tutorials and FAQs
    Tutorials are now available on topics such as finding a gene, exploring homology, getting information on gene expression, and navigating MGI. There is a Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) page.
