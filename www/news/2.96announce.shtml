MGI Release 2.96 Announcement - May 19, 2003  

MGI is pleased to announce its 2.96 release.

  • We want to hear from you! Please take the time to respond to our survey and help us improve MGI resources.


  • Our new Adult Anatomy Browser lets you browse or search an ontology describing the anatomy of the adult mouse (Theiler Stage 28). The Browser structure is similar to that of the Gene Ontology (GO) and of the Mammalian Phenotypes (MP). See Vocabulary Browsers for more information.


  • A new feature on the Genes and Markers Query Form provides an option to query by Interpro terms. Any resultant Interpro associations appear on marker detail pages.


  • You can also query for markers and clones by IMAGE and RIKEN clone sets from the Genes and Markers Query Form, the Molecular Probes and Clones Query Form, and the cDNA Clone Query Form.


  • A query from the Genes and Markers Query Form now returns any markers associated (via SWISS-PROT records) with Protein Data Bank (PDB) accession IDs.
