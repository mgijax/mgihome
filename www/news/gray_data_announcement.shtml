Major Transcription Factor Study Contributes to GXD  

GXD is pleased to announce the inclusion of data from two major in situ hybridization studies.

  • Dr. Paul Gray and colleagues examined the expression of 1333 putative transcription factors in the developing mouse nervous system. The contributed data set is published in:
    Gray PA; Fu H; Luo P; Zhao Q; Yu J; Ferrari A; Tenzen T; Yuk DI; Tsung EF; Cai Z; Alberta JA; Cheng LP; Liu Y; Stenman JM; Valerious MT; Billings N; Kim HA; Greenberg ME; McMahon AP; Rowitch DH; Stiles CD; Ma Q. Mouse Brain Organization Revealed Through Direct Genome Scale TF Expression Analysis. 2004. Science 306:2255-2257.
    You can view the data set at accession number J:91257. This set includes:

    • 1333 genes
    • 1505 probes
    • 2766 assays (in situ hybridizations at E10.5, E13.5 and P0 stages)

  • Dr. Seth Blackshaw and colleagues examined the expression of # of genes in the developing retina. The data set is published in:

    You can view the data set at accession number J:93300. This set includes:

    • # genes
    • # probes
    • # assays (in situ hybridizations at E12.5, E14.5, E16.5, E18.5, P0-1, P2-3, P4-5, P6-7, P8-10 and Adult stages)
