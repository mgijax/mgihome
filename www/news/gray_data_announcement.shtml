Major Transcription Factor Study Contributes to GXD  

GXD is pleased to announce the inclusion of a major study examining the expression of 1329 putative transcription factors in the developing mouse nervous system. It was contributed by Dr. Paul Gray and colleagues and is published in:

Gray PA; Fu H; Luo P; Zhao Q; Yu J; Ferrari A; Tenzen T; Yuk D; Tsung EF; Cai Z; Alberta JA; Cheng L; Liu Y; Stenman JM; Valerious MT; Billings N; Kim HA; Greenberg ME; McMahon AP; Rowitch DH; Stiles CD; Ma Q. Mouse Brain Organization Revealed through Direct Genome Scale Transcription Factor Expression Analysis. 2004 Science, in press.

You can view the data set at accession number J:91257.

This data set includes:

  • 1329 genes
  • 1500 probes
  • 2582 assays (in situ hybridizations at E10.5, E13.5 and P0)
  • 44131 results
