MIT Release #9 Mouse Genetic Maps Now Available


February 28, 1995

The January 1995 release of the Whitehead Institute/MIT Center for Genome Research mouse genetic maps is now available. The maps comprise 6252 polymorphic mouse microsatellite repeats mapped on a C57BL/6J-ob x CAST F2 intercross using PCR probes.

Data from this release have been incorporated into the locus information tables AND the PCR primer tables of the Mouse Genome Database (MGD). The new data will be reflected in any information related to MIT markers retrieved from the database.

Data from these reports have also been incorporated into a new set of MIT data files for the Encyclopedia of the Mouse Genome.

You may download these from the Encyclopedia Home Page on the World Wide Web or from our anonymous ftp server.

The ftp directory is pub/encyclo/data/3.0/mit.jan.1995
