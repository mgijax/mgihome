MGI Release 3.41 Announcement - December xx, 2005  

MGI is pleased to announce the following new features in its 3.41 release:

  • An electronic version of Origins of Inbred Mice edited by Herbert C. Morse III. This book, originally published in 1978 and long out of print, provides an historical perspective on the development of inbred mice. It is thoroughly indexed and linked to appropriate terms at MGI. This book joins the electronic versions of three other out-of-print books, The Anatomy of the Laboratory Mouse by Margaret J. Cook, Mouse Genetics by Lee Silver and The Coat Colors of Mice by Willys K. Silvers. Links to all four appear on Additional MGI Tools and Links. We hope that you find this book useful. Please send questions and comments to User Support.


  • The remainder of the MGI 3.41 release consists of performance enhancements and bug fixes.
