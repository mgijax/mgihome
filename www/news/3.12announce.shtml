MGI Release 3.11 Announcement - April, 2005  

MGI is pleased to announce its 3.12 release.

  • We've incorporated the Gene Ontology (GO) classifications into a new Gene Ontology Annotation Detail page. A paragraph of text about a given gene now precedes the classifications table. See GO annotations for a sample description of Pax6. To access this information, enter your gene of interest on the Genes and Markers Query Form/Gene; on the Gene Detail page that appears, click an item in the Gene Ontology (GO) Classifications field.

  • The remainder of the MGI 3.12 release consists of performance enhancements and bug fixes.
