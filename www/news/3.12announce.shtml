MGI Release 3.12 Announcement - April 8, 2005  

MGI is pleased to announce its 3.12 release.

  • MGI version 3.12 provides new information on Gene Ontology (GO) Annotation Detail pages.

    • A paragraph of annotation summary text appears prior to the GO classifications. These texts are generated from structured GO annotations appearing in the table below them on these detail pages.

    • GO terms are highlighted in the paragraph and linked to relevant MGI marker detail and reference pages as well as to databases containing additional data.

    • To access this information, enter a gene of interest in the Symbol/Name field on the Genes and Markers Query Form (use the is operator rather than the default, begins) and click Search. On the summary page that appears, click the gene of interest. On gene detail page, click All GO Classifications. The Gene Ontology Classifications page with annotation summary text about your gene appears.

  • You can now include dog when performing a two-species orthology comparison using the Oxford Grid Query Form. Cells in the resulting grid represent a comparison of two chromosomes, one from each of the selected species. The grid includes links to comparative maps.

  • The remainder of the MGI 3.12 release consists of performance enhancements and bug fixes.
