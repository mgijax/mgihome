Have you registered?

If you have not filled out our User Registration form, please do so. Your registration enables User Support to assist our user community more efficiently. In addition, registered users receive news and updates about MGI information resources.

Reporting a Problem

You can use the MGI User Support Express Mail form to report a problem or send us your questions, comments and suggestions.

When reporting a problem, please include the following information in your message:

Name of the query form in use when error occurred
Error messages, if any
The procedure you used
The desired results
The actual results
Description of incorrect data, if any
Browser
Type of computer

Comments and Feedback

Please send us your ideas, wishes, comments and suggestions. We want to know how we can improve our information resources to serve you better. Thanks for taking the time to help us.

MGI E-mail List Service

MGI maintains an E-mail list service to promote communication among researchers on a variety of issues. MGI-LIST, with over 2200 subscribers, is the mouse community bulletin board. PHENOME-LIST, created in August 2000, provides a forum for strain characterization and phenotypic data collection. Subscribe now, and network with your research community.
