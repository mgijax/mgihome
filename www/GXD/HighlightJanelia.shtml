Janelia Mouse Atlas

In toto imaging and reconstruction of post-implantation mouse development at the single-cell level. McDole et al. 2018 Cell 175(3):859-876.

Movie S27 | Joint visualization of dynamic cell fate reconstruction and cell divisions across a developing embryo.
Combination of the data on cell divisions identified by the CNN-based detector with SVF-enhanced cell tracking and manually annotated tissue labels. Jointly visualizing these databases reveals cell fate information (color), cell movements (spheres) and cell divisions in each tissue (change in size of spheres, maximum size identifies time point of division), as a function of time. Time is represented in the format hh:mm:ss.

Additional movies are available at the Janelia website.
