The Gene Expression + Phenotype Comparison Matrix, accessible from gene detail pages, allows users to compare gene expression and phenotype data for a given gene. It visually juxtaposes tissues where a gene is normally expressed against tissues where mutations in that gene cause abnormalities. The anatomy axis of the view can be expanded and collapsed, allowing interactive exploration of the correlations between gene expression and phenotype at different levels of detail.
