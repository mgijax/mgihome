New interface utilities have been developed to help users find recombinase (cre) reporters with activity in specific tissues. The new Gene Expression and Recombinase Activity Comparison Matrix (upper) juxtaposes the activity patterns for recombinases driven by the mouse gene promoter against the endogenous expression pattern of the gene. The matrix view is accessible from relevant gene and allele detail pages, as well as from the recombinase allele search summary. In addition, the Recombinase (cre) Activity Search (lower) now allows users to narrow their query to reporters whose activity has been detected in the specified structure (or its substructures) but nowhere else.
