GO Curators  

The following groups provide GO annotations for mouse genes:

  • MGI - experimental-literature-based annotation to mouse gene products, as well as electronic-based annotation by translation of UniProt keywords, Interpro Domains, and EC numbers.

  • UniProt (Universal Protein Resource) - protein annotation from many organisms in UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot Protein Knowledgebase (a protein sequence database).

  • HGNC (HUGO Gene Nomenclature Committee) - approved nomenclature for human genes and close collaboration with MGI in establishing nomenclature for mouse genes. Through HUGOA (Human Gene Ontology Annotation}, the knowledge base used to assign nomenclature is applied to GO annotaton.

  • IntAct (Protein Interaction Group at EBI) - literature-curated protein interaction data.

  • PINC (Proteome Incorporated) - annotations created in 2001 for NCBI and extracted from EntrezGene.

  • RI (Roslin Institute) - annotation through several database systems, including ArkDB, maintained at their site.
