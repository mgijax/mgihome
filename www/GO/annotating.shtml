Annotating to GO at MGI

MGI uses database-mining and other strategies to automate gene annotation. The electronic annotations are attributed to MGI references J:56000, J:60000.

In addition, scientific curators review the published literature and extract information to manually annotate gene records. The annotated records include the supporting references.

Automated annotation strategies include:
  • mapping the Enzyme Commission (EC) number associated with a gene to the EC number attached to GO terms
  • translating SWISS-PROT "Keywords" associated with a gene to appropriate GO terms
  • associating GO terms to a gene based upon proper gene nomenclature; for example, G-protein coupled receptor genes can be annotated as:
    • Molecular Function: G protein linked receptor
    • Cellular Component: integral plasma membrane protein
    • Biological Process: G protein linked receptor signalling pathway
If you have questions, need assistance, or wish to report a problem with specific annotations, please contact MGI at mgi-help@informatics.jax.org.
