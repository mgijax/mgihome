MGI E-mail List Service

MGI maintains e-mail lists for the genetics research community. MGI uses Mail-List.com which is hosted at The Jackson Laboratory. MGI manages and moderates the lists.

Click the links below to the list archives. You can visit these lists as a non-member to see if you want to subscribe. You can also search the list archives for discussions that may have been covered in the past. (Note: We switched to Mail-List.com in February 2020, and the archives now begin as of then.) Once you subscribe, you can manage your subscription via a link in email footers.

  • View MGI-LIST - Forum for topics in mouse genetics and MGI news.
  • View MGI-TECHNICAL-LIST - Forum for technical information about accessing MGI data, using APIs, and making links to MGI.
