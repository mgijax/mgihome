MGI E-mail List Service

MGI maintains several E-mail lists for the genetics research community. The list service uses the Lyris list service software package, version 3.0. To get information and instructions regarding the list service send a message containing the word help in the subject or message body to
lists@lists.informatics.jax.org

The lists currently available are

  • MGI-LIST - Forum for topics in mouse genetics, MGI news updates
  • RAT-LIST - Forum for rat genetics

You can click on a list name above to get to the main menu for the list. You can visit the list as a non-member to see if the discussions interest you and then decide whether you want to subscribe.

How to Subscribe

You can subscribe to one or both lists, here. Enter your name and E-mail address below and check the list(s) you want to subscribe to. Click on the Submit button to send your subscription. You will receive an acknowledgement and instructions via E-mail.
