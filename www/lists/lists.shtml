MGI E-mail List Service

MGI maintains e-mail lists for the genetics research community. MGI uses a hosting service, Dundee Internet, who maintains the hardware and Lyris version 12 software for the lists. MGI manages and moderates the lists.

Click the link below to get to the list on the main menu. You can visit these lists as a non-member to see if you want to subscribe. You can also search the list archives for discussions that may have been covered in the past. Once you subscribe, you can manage your subscription (for example, to read or create messages or to unsubscribe) using the web version of the list.

  • Enter MGI-LIST - Forum for topics in mouse genetics, MGI news updates.
  • Enter MGI-TECHNICAL-LIST - Forum for technical information about accessing MGI data, using APIs, and making links to MGI.
