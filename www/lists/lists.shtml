MGI E-mail List Service

MGI uses the Alliance Community Forum for mouse genetic research community discussions and to announce updates to MGI. Released in August 2021, the Alliance Community Forum, is a discussion forum for researchers interested in being informed about Alliance news and updates. The Alliance Community Forum also represents the first place for discussions across the Alliance and seven model organism communities. Members can also post job openings, ask questions, and participate in discussions on genomic resources and tools. The Alliance Community Forum replaces mgi-list, used for several decades for discussion and announcements.

MGI maintains a separate e-mail forum for technical information about accessing MGI data, using APIs, and making links to MGI, the mgi-technical-list. For this, MGI uses Mail-List.com which is hosted at The Jackson Laboratory. MGI manages and moderates the technical list.

Click the links below to view the technical-list archives, as well as the former mgi-list archives.

  • View MGI-LIST - Forum for topics in mouse genetics and MGI news.
  • View MGI-TECHNICAL-LIST - Forum for technical information about accessing MGI data, using APIs, and making links to MGI.
