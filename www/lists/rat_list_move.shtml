RAT-LIST Update, January 21, 2001



Rat-list has joined the Rat Community Forum (RCF) at the Rat Genome Database.

The two rat forums (rat-list and RCF) have been merged into one.
The rat-list server at this site is being discontinued and all its subscribers have been pre-subscribed to RCF.
You can subscribe/unsubscribe, read messages, and search the RCF archives from the RCF configuration page .

For those interested in mouse, rodent, and mammalian genetics, we continue to support mgi-list and encourage you to participate in this forum.
