MGI Mirror Sites  
MGI mirror sites provide alternative locations from which to access MGI data resources, including MGD and GXD. All sites are fully operational and are updated on a regular basis.

Information on setting up an MGI mirror site is available via anonymous ftp:

  • For technical requirements, see ftp://www.informatics.jax.org/outgoing/mgd/tech-spec.pdf.
  • For installation instructions, see ftp://www.informatics.jax.org/outgoing/mgd/installdoc.pdf.
