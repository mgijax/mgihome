Computational Genome Annotation Systems

These projects annotate genomic sequence based on computational methods.
  • Genome Annotation Consortium (ORNL) - Human, mouse, yeast and prokaryotic genomes.
  • EnsEMBL - (The Sanger Center and EBI) - Currently, only human.

Curated Model Organism Databases

These databases contain information on features in genomic sequence curated from literature.
  • MGD/GXD/MGS (The Jackson Laboratory) - Mus musculus
  • GDB (supported by the Research Triangle Institute) (RTI) - Homo sapiens
  • FlyBase (BDGP, EDGP, Harvard University, Indiana University) - Drosophila manogastar
  • SGD (Stanford University) - Saccharomyces cerevisiae
  • TAIR (Stanford University) - Arabidopsis thaliana
  • ACEDB (The Sanger Center) - C. elegans
