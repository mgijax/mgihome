Mouse Genome Sequencing Initiative Progress

The working draft of the mouse genome is scheduled for completion in the spring of 2001. This working draft will cover 90% of the genome at 3X coverage. The draft sequence will have many gaps, but will be useful for identifying most of the genes encoded in the mouse genome.

The complete, finished mouse genome should be available no later than the spring of 2005.
