Mouse Nomenclature Home Page  

The Mouse Genome Informatics Database is the authoritative source of official names for mouse genes, alleles, and strains. Nomenclature follows the rules and guidelines established by the International Committee on Standardized Genetic Nomenclature for Mice and is implemented through the Mouse Genomic Nomenclature Committee (MGNC).
