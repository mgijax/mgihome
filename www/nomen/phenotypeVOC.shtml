Phenotype Classification Terms

The following table lists a subset of terms from the MGI phenotype vocabulary and consists of the broadest, high-level terms. These terms are being applied to phenotypes in the Mouse Genome Database (MGD) and are used to assist in searching, grouping, comparing and analyzing phenotype data. These terms are also being used by a number of the Mutagenesis Centers.

In addition to the classification terms, the table provides examples and term definitions. A larger more complete vocabulary is under development. We seek user input on the usefulness of these phenotype classification terms and encourage experts, in particular systems and diseases, to participate in developing the full hierarchy of phenotype terms. For more information contact User Support.
