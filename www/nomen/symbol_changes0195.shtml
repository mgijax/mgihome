Nomenclature Symbol Changes - January 1995

The International Mouse Nomenclature Committee has approved changing mutant locus symbols to be alleles, as the genes responsible for the mutations are identified. For example Tfm will be withdrawn and become an allele of Ar, Ar<Tfm>. This is the list of changes as of January 1995.
