MGI works well with most browsers. We know of no issues with fairly recent versions of
  • Chrome,
  • Firefox
    • or
  • Safari
browsers. Some of the more advanced features of MGI, MouseMine and JBrowse may not work properly with IE 8 or 9.

For MGI features to perform as designed, JavaScript needs to be enabled. You can do this in your browser's Preferences (Mac) or Settings/Internet Options (PC).

If you have an issue with one browser that is resolved when you switch to different browser, please report this to our User Support Group.
