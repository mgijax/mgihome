MGI provides a curated set of mammalian orthologs for the research community. MGI focuses on integration of orthology sets from sequenced mammalian genomes (e.g., human, rat, dog, chimp). This set is constructed through an iterative process using both computational and manual approaches. We work with the HomoloGene resource at the NCBI to incorporate some of the HomoloGene computational three-way reciprocal best-hit sets into the MGI system. HomoloGene incorporates MGI-curated mammalian orthology sets in their resources. In addition, we continue to work with the research community to carefully curate gene family sets, usually at the instigation of the research community.
