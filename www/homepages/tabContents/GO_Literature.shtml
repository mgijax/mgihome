GO Consortium bibliography at http://www.geneontology.org/cgi-bin/biblio.cgi
     Here you can access over 1700 of the papers written about the GO, or about the use of the GO and GO annotation sets for data retrieval and analysis.

Current MGI Favorites:

  • Lein, E.S., et al., 2007. Genome-wide atlas of gene expression in the adult mouse brain. Nature 445:168-176. [PMID: 17151600]
         Uses GO to analyze functional organization of the brain at the cellular (rather than tissue) level.

  • Sjöblom, T., et al., 2006. The Consensus Coding Sequences of Human Breast and Colorectal Cancers. Science 314: 268-274. [PMID: 16959974]
         Uses GO characterization to classify functional sets of candidate cancer genes.

  • Kislinger T., et al., 2003. PRISM, A Generic Large Scale Proteomic Investigation Strategy for Mammals. Mole Cell Proteomics 2: 96-106. [PMID: 12644571]
         Develops an automated clustering method to identify GO annotation groups for streamlined analysis that revealed spatial specificity of mammalian proteins.
