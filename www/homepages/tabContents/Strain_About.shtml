Much data in MGI relates to the C57BL/6J reference strain, MGI contains other strain information including:

  • Strain distribution patterns for recombinant inbred (RI) and recombinant congenic (RC) strains and cross haplotypes
  • Polymorphic loci related to specified strains
  • SNPs and other sequence polymorphisms in one strain or differences between many strains
  • Information on inbred strains (M. Festing's listing)
  • Phenotypic characteristics of strains and of mutant alleles on a variety of genetic background strains
  • Information about the strains of clones from which sequences are derived
