• GXD is a community resource for gene expression information from the laboratory mouse.
  • GXD currently collects detailed expression data from the following assay types:  RNA in situ hybridization; Immunohistochemistry; in situ reporter (knock in); RT-PCR; Northern and Western blots; and RNase protection and Nuclease S1 protection assays.
  • GXD also maintains an index of the literature examining gene expression in the embryonic mouse.  It is comprehensive and up-to-date, containing all pertinent journal articles from 1993 to the present and articles from major developmental journals from 1990 to the present.
  • GXD is funded by NIH/NICHD grant HD033745
