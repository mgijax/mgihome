General Help

Mouse Genome Informatics offers on-line help and staffs a help desk to answer your questions and comments. Please feel free to contact User Support for any of the following:

  • You can't find the data you're looking for
  • You've found an error on one of our web pages
  • You have a paper you'd like us to curate
  • You need us to create a special report
  • You have data you'd like to submit
  • You have a complaint about or a suggestion for our website

You can reach us by email, by submitting our Express Mail form , or at (207) 288-6445.
