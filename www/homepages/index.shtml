MGI news Release 4.0 Jan 2008
  • The MGI website and navigation tools have been redesigned. Check above for major areas to explore and try the pull-down toolbar to quickly find the page or search form you are looking for. Comments and feedback are welcome.
  • MouseCyc, a new database of curated biochemical pathways for the laboratory mouse, is now available from the MGI website.
  • Named mouse strains with their MGI accession IDs and official nomenclature can now be searched and downloaded.
