Gene Expression Database (GXD)
GXD collects and integrates the gene expression information in MGI. Its primary emphasis is on endogenous gene expression during mouse development.

Gene Expression News 19 Nov 2007
GXD is pleased to announce the inclusion of in situ hybridization data from Dr. Gregor Eichele of the GenePaint database [www.genepaint.org; Visel A; Thaller C; Eichele G. GenePaint.org: an atlas of gene expression patterns in the mouse embryo. 2004. Nucleic Acids Research 32(Database issue):D552-6]. The data set can be viewed at accession number J:122989. This set includes over 69,000 results; 745 genes; 1,397 probes; 748 assays (in situ hybridizations at E14.5); and 10,585 images.
